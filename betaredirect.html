How to Migrate your VPS to Live

All users will have a 30 day grace period to migrate their VPS to live. To start a new machine, simply navigate to SkySilk.com and login with your beta credentials to access the live platform. The process only takes a few clicks and you will receive a link to your new user dashboard.

Will I be Charged Automatically?

You will be prompted when migrating your machine to live to confirm monthly payments. The monthly pricing will be based on the resources of the VPS you are migrating.
